Welcome to Matcha! To ensure a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all users, we have established the following rules. By using our platform, you agree to comply with these rules.

1. Respectful Interaction

Treat all users with kindness and respect.

No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying of any kind.

Avoid making offensive or inappropriate comments.

Respect users' boundaries and privacy.

2. Authenticity and Honesty

Users must provide accurate and truthful information about themselves.

Impersonation of others is strictly prohibited.

Catfishing, misrepresentation, or fraudulent activity will result in account termination.

3. Profile Guidelines

Profile photos must be clear, recent, and appropriate.

Do not upload explicit, offensive, or copyrighted content.

Profiles must not contain false, misleading, or promotional information.

4. Communication Rules

All communication must remain respectful and appropriate.

No spamming, unsolicited messages, or repeated contact without consent.

Discussions of illegal activities, violence, or abuse are not allowed.

Users must not share personal contact information publicly.

5. Prohibited Content

No promotion of illegal activities, drugs, or harmful substances.

No content that infringes on intellectual property rights.

6. Privacy and Safety

Do not share your login credentials with others.

Avoid sharing personal details such as home addresses, financial information, or passwords.

Report any suspicious activity or inappropriate behavior.

Matcha will never ask for your password or sensitive information outside the platform.

7. Account Security

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their accounts.

Any unauthorized access or breaches must be reported immediately.

Users must use strong passwords and avoid using the same password across different platforms.

8. No Commercial Use

Users may not use Matcha for commercial purposes without explicit authorization.

Soliciting other users for business, marketing, or promotional purposes is prohibited.

9. Reporting Violations

If you encounter any violation of these rules, report it to our support team.

Matcha reserves the right to take appropriate action, including warning, suspension, or banning of accounts.

10. Compliance with Laws

Users must comply with all applicable local, state, and federal laws.

Any illegal activity conducted on Matcha will be reported to the authorities.

11. Account Termination

Matcha reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these rules.

Users who repeatedly violate the rules will be permanently banned.

12. Updates to Rules

Matcha may update these rules periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated rules.

13. Contact Us
For any questions or concerns regarding these rules, please contact us.
